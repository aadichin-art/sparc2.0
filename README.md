A website built to foster connections between students on campus and get them more engaged. Groups of four are randomly assigned to one another and given prompts meant to encourage discussions between them. If a group member is not satisfied with their group they are able to skip and get put into another group of four. Students sign up using their school university email address as a way to verify that they actually go there. There is a report option in case of inappropriate behavior from another group member that gets sent to be under review. Every three days groups get randomized again. Every 12 hours a new question appears as a conversation starter. These groups can be a way to connect to others and find companionship or even for mentorship.
